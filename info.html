Want to know what we are all about?

Below you can find information about the festival.

BEFORE YOUR ARRIVAL

Please remember to announce your arrival, when you are coming. That way we will always have an overview of which bands have arrived – and your performance slot won’t be given away to a band on the waiting list. At the same time you will get assigned your Guard-time. There is meet up 30 mins before your performance supposed to start, just behind the stage where you will perform. There will be staff to assist you behind the scenes. If you are in doubt how to find the stage and back-stage, you can always acquire information at the check in/information.

PARKING

Large busses (over 9 pers.) can park at Teglgårds Plads on the East side ofNordkraft (as long as there is space) - parking licenses can be obtained at Check In. Smaller busses will be guided to other nearby parking places. See Nordkrafts website (www.nordkraft.dk) for info about nearby parking places. On most spots there is free parking from Saturday afternoon.

SMOKING/DRINKING

It needs to be underlined that bringing or consuming beer/wine/spirits/weed is strictly forbidden. It is valid both in and outside of Nordkraft. It is the responsibility of the accompanying staff that participants respect these rules. Consumption of the above-mentioned substances will result in the club being expelled from the festival.

WARDROBES / TOILETS

There will be a possibility to use Skråens wardrobes for your outerwear, and we will attempt to establish an instrument storage likewise at Skråen.

SAFETY

The participating youth clubs and schools need to contribute a guard for 2 hours/ bands joined. Planning of the guarding timetable at this point have started. To begin with, every participating club will take on a 2 hour long duty.
