ABOUT THE SCENES

Royal Stage is big! Here we have an enormous stage, sound- and light system. The size of the audience can be up to 1.300! If you dare, come and take this magestic stage!!

Skråen is a very attractive club stage. We are talking about a big and good looking “play-anywhere” set up with the capacity of up to 500 visitors.

Scene Joule is built for this occasion in one of the most attractive theatre halls in Nordkraft. Stage, light and sound quality is top notch and there is space for ca. 150 audience members.

Scene Watt is also built for the occasion in one of Nordkraft’s awesome halls. There is space for about 150 audience members.

Baghuset is located in a cozy and atmospheric setting, which is part of the cultural and musical scene in Aalborg on a daily basis. All un-plugged performances to take place here. There is space for about 60 people a the stage of Baghuset.

Kulkælderscenen is located in the cellar. It is a mega cool stage, which is perfectly suitable for hip hop, e-music, rap, etc. This stage is hosting all electronic performances! There is space for ca. 150 people.
