BIGGEST YOUTH FESTIVAL IN THE NORDIC REGION

Ungdomsringens Musikfestival is Denmark’s largest festival for freetime, youth club and youth school bands. It will be held on the 3rd weekend in January, 15-16 January, 2021.

The goal of the festival is to provide the chance to experience a fun and adventurous weekend in Aalborg with music in focus. Everybody is welcome!

BECOME A PART OF SOMETHING GREAT

Join your fellow like-minded young musicians in a unique adventure! Find new friends who share your passions, in an open and judgement-free community.

We hope that you will fill busses and bring your whole club or class with you to Aalborg. Free entry for all guests!
