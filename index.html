BIGGEST YOUTH FESTIVAL IN THE NORDIC REGION

Ungdomsringens Musikfestival is Denmark’s largest festival for freetime, youth club and youth school bands. Every year it is held on the 3rd weekend in January.

Experience a fun and adventurous weekend in Aalborg with music in focus. Everybody is welcome!

BECOME A PART OF SOMETHING GREAT

Join your fellow like-minded young musicians in a unique adventure! Come and take over the stage, find new friends who share your passions, in an inspiring and inclusive community.

The festival is substance-free, smoking, consumption of alcohol and drugs is prohibited.

We hope that you will fill busses and bring your whole club or class with you to Aalborg. Free entry for all guests!

UNGDOMSRINGENS'
STARS RISING TO FAME

UM is more than just any festival. We have seen our performers succeed, including spectacular bands like ‘Nephew’ and ‘The Minds of 99’.

So take the opportunity and show your talent, as now is the time for future stars to shine!
